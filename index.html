About Us

Globetrotter is a place for tourists who love to travel and to
gather knowledge about the place they visit. The important and
most entertaining part of traveling is to collect knowledge of
local traditions and history of the place. We help you to
pre-plan your trip and in addition to that, we connect you to
all the local guides so that just like cabs you can book your
guides in advance.
